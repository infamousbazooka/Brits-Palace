ABOUT US

Brit’s Palace is the ultimate mansion for luxury living in Goa. Completed in 2016, this divine palatial house is unlike anything you’ve seen. Built across an area of more than 1500 sq.mts. with impressive amenities, nature & skyline views, we at Brit's Palace have expertly created this luxurious abode that will not only exceed the standards of quality but will also deliver unexpected details and comforts.

Brit’s Palace is located in a central business area but in a quiet neighbourhood, offering you serenity and tranquil, galore. Our swimming pool (with a kid’s pool too) is the centrepiece of our charming garden and is set among lush green trees with gentle birds chirping all day. Our garden is ideal for BBQs and family get-togethers. Brit’s Palace also boasts of a badminton court, allowing you to keep fit and active even while on vacation.

For the daring and brave side of you, Brit’s Palace offers to help you discover a different side of Goa. From adventure sports, mountain climbing, wild life treks, go-carting, river rafting, paragliding and a whole lot more. So whether you’re interest in Goan history & the rich culture or if you simply love relaxing on a sunny beach with cool breeze in your hair, sand in your toes and a drink in hand, you’re at the right place at the right time.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simple; to offer the world’s best in luxury living spaces. With a strong management team and industry professionals, we aim to give you the finest experience, one which includes living like royalty. At Brit’s Palace you can be assured of the best deals during your stay here. Our team is constantly improving in order to stay on top of the table and provide the best client experience. Brit’s Palace is your key to Live like Royalty.
